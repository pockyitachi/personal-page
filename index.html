02 — About

Research at the intersection of AI and software systems.

My work focuses on intelligent mobile software systems — designing agents that combine large language models, vision-language pipelines, and finite-state reasoning to automate and test complex mobile applications, and to stay state-aware, robust, and recoverable when things go wrong.

Before my PhD I built and maintained microservices for customer personalization and recommendation at Walmart Global Tech, integrating GenAI into search and tuning Milvus on Kubernetes. I hold an MS in ECE (AI track) from the University of Miami and a BS in Electrical Engineering from Missouri.
