01 — About

Research at the intersection of AI & software systems

My work focuses on intelligent mobile software systems — designing agents that combine large language models, vision-language pipelines, and finite-state reasoning to automate and test complex mobile applications. I care about making these systems state-aware, robust, and recoverable when things go wrong.

Before my PhD, I built and maintained microservices for customer personalization and recommendation at Walmart Global Tech, integrating GenAI workflows into search and working with Milvus on Kubernetes. I hold an MS in ECE (AI track) from the University of Miami and a BS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Missouri.

05 — Projects

Selected projects

Face Recognition System

May 2022

Compared Deep Learning vs. Bag-of-Visual-Words for face recognition. Modified GoogLeNet with custom FC/output layers via transfer learning; built a multi-SVM classifier over a BoVW codebook. Achieved 83.5% (DL) vs. 58.5% (BoVW) accuracy.

  • Deep Learning
  • Computer Vision
  • Transfer Learning

Online Ticket Ordering System

Jun 2021

Microservice architecture in Spring with HTTP communication and a React front-end for searching events and viewing recommendations. MySQL/MongoDB for CRUD; an ML-based recommender driven by user preferences.

  • React
  • MySQL

Color Image Enhancement

Oct 2021

Digital image processing pipeline for feature matching: sharpening (high-boost & Laplacian), per-channel Gaussian denoising, histogram equalization for tone correction, and homomorphic filtering for illumination adjustment.

  • Image Processing
  • OpenCV
  • Filtering

Game Outcome Prediction

Apr 2021

Predicted match winners from a ~9,900-game League of Legends dataset (47 attributes). Cleaned to ~7,000 samples / 30 attributes, then compared SVM, Decision Tree, and Logistic Regression performance.

Real-time data collection & transmission for a Mizzou Space Program rocket (launched May 2018). Integrated temperature/humidity/pressure sensors, an IMU and GPS over I2C, an RF transceiver/receiver via Arduino, and an SD backup system.
